Voyager Golden Record T-Shirts
& other cool space merch

Original and unique T-shirts featuring designs inspired by the Golden Records carried by NASA's two Voyager probes. Each record contained imformation about Earth and humanity, and the design of these T-shirts is the instructions for how an alien intelligence would decode the data. Launched in 1977, the Voyager satellites were sent into space from Cape Canaveral, Florida to explore the outer reaches of our solar system. On August 25, 2012, data from Voyager 1 indicated that it had become the first human-made object to enter interstellar space, traveling further than anyone, or anything, in history.
